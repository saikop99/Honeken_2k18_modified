    • ABOUT OBSEQUIO

    Obsequio is a competition about presenting a powerpoint presentation on entitled topics. It’s all about how deep you have understood the topic,how you present your topic in front of audience.We see the way you present it, the way you put your information, in short, the style of presenting the paper. This event is aimed at boosting the technological and communication skills inherent in today's youth, also providing them with an opportunity to exhibit their innovative ideas and thoughts.This event encompasses all the domains within the horizon of technology. Obsequio rather encourages interaction thereby facilitating transformation of seedling ideas to giant trees of innovation and progress. We hope that our endeavor will play a significant part in providing the platform for the deserving minds on an ever rising and never ending growth

    RULES
    • For a team a maximum of 3 members are allowed

    • Teams must not contain male and female participants together

    • Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should be a maximum of 6 pages

    • The paper must strictly follow IEEE format

    • College ID card is a must for the participants

    • Please bring your hard copy both on CD and pen drive

    • Mail the paper to honeken2k16@gmail.com

    • After submitting the soft copy, a panel of judges will go through your material and if you are shortlisted to give presentation, you will be notified

    • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained
