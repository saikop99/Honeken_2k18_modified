    • ABOUT ROBOTHON

    The competition is all about perfection and efficiency. Finish the track as fast as possible, and tackle the obstacles and survive the track and all this with the clock ticking. Build a manually controlled BOT which uses mechanically or electrically operated wheels and make the BOT to race. The control may be wired or wireless network."Join the game, Earn the fame."

    Description & RULES

    Description of Arena

    Arena will not be displayed earlier. Info regarding arena is as follows:
    • The track may consists of obstacles like:


    1. Gravel with obstacles
    2. Valleys, see-saw
    3. Steps, slopes, rotating elements.

    • The time limit is a maximum of 5 minutes.
    • Width of the track is 35 cm
    • Penalties are imposed
    RULES
    • Maximum of three members per team

    • The BOT size should be of dimension of 25 * 25 * 25 cm.( Tolerance of +/- 3cm will be permitted ).

    • BOT should not damage the arena

    • BOT should go through all the obstacles and reach the end line

    • Maximum of 12 V potential drop and 350 rpm on any point of the BOT.

    • External battery power supply is permitted

    • BOT can be wired or wireless. If wired, wires should be more than 3 to 4 metres. If wireless, atleast two different frequencies should be brought.

    • Guidelines mentioned are subjected to change

    • Judges decision will be considered final

    • Organizers are not responsible for damage of BOT in track obstacle
