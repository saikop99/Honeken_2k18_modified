    RULES

    Topics
  • LEVEL 1: PRELIMINARY ROUND
    1. There will be 15 Questions for 20 minutes.
    2. Batches will be selected for the final round depending on the score.

  • LEVEL 2: FINAL ROUND
    1. The bug circuit should be debugged and the output should be shown along with explanation.
    2. All the components will be provided.
    3. No external components are allowed.
    4. If there is any damage to components, the batch will be disqualified.
    5. Time taken to complete the debugging will also be considered and accordingly the prizes will be announced.
    6. Decision of the judge will be final.


    • For a team a maximum of 2 members are allowed

    • Participants should provide the valid ID card.

    • Usage of electronic devices other than calculators is prohibited.
